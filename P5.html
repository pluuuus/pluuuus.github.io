Urban Wildlife Food Dispenser


For the public installation, I worked with Carolina and we make a urban wildlife feeder. The function of it is when a passenger go by and throw some garbage into a trash bin, the feeder which connected to the trash bin will give some bird/squirrel food that allowed people to feed the urban wildlife. The goal of this project is 1. Enhance human-animal interactions; 2. Provide an incentive to help maintain a public park clean; 3. Supply a source of beneficial nutrition for urban wildlife that was actually healthy for those interested in feeding the animals.
